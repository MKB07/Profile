# Biological Sciences Experience

My main scientific field is human genetics. I have worked with family-history interpretation, inheritance models, genetic risk assessment, and communication of genetic information in clinical settings.

## Clinical Domains

Experience includes:

- pediatric genetics;
- oncogenetics;
- cardiogenetics;
- prenatal genetics;
- preconception counseling;

## Genetic Counseling Practice

My clinical genetics work involved:

- collecting and structuring family history;
- building and interpreting pedigrees;
- explaining inheritance mechanisms;
- discussing genetic testing and possible implications;
- preparing cases for multidisciplinary review;
- maintaining clinical records and follow-up information;
- supporting communication between patients, clinicians and laboratory teams.
